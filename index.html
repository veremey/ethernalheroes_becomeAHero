Game
description

The world of Ethernium is torn apart by an endless war that is destroying everything in its path. The insidious, merciless, legions of invincible demons are preparing to wipe out all life from the face of the earth. In an era of endless war, survival depends only on your courage and winning strategy

Choose one of the four factions!

Become the leader of the mysterious night elves, the elder of the crafty magicians, the ruler of the ruthless demons, or head the Order of the Right Inquisitors, watching the bloody battles collapse alliances and kill entire nations.

Call the hero to lead your army!

Let your troops fight side by side with the strongest heroes. Hardened in battle brave, whose level grows with each battle, can use spells, strengthen troops and inflict horror on your enemies.

Take part in the epic events!

Before your eyes will pass the events of the whole historical era of Ethernium.

During the passage of the story you will learn about the mystery of the opening of the gates between the worlds, the tragic destruction of the kingdom of elves, the treachery of the inquisitor Erinyes and the battle of all the kingdoms at the gates of anger.

The total volume of the CCDG market

The CCDG market has more than 40 million active players worldwide, most of which live in the US and Asia, where the genre is traditionally popular. The genre is characterized by a high average check per player and a stable loyal audience.

The total volume of the CCDG market is 1.4 billion USD. And all this amount of money is spent on buying boosters and pumping cards. At the same time, according to statistics, only 4000 pro-players all over the world earn money due to their skills in games.

Token
functions

By analogy with poker rooms, part of each player's bet of 2% will be held in the form of a platform commission.

From these commissions will be formed a bonus pool, which will be played monthly in paid and free tournaments.

The main feature of Ethernal Heroes is the ability to implement all types of in-game transactions in HERO tokens that comply with the ERC-20 standard. What are the HERO tokens for:

  • Bets between players in games 1-on-1

  • Payment for participation in tournaments held on the platform

  • Possibility of input / output of HERO tokens to exchanges

  • Buying boosters and game content

  • Using in-game in-game items for transactions between players
